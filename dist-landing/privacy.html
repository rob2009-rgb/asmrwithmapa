Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ASMR with MAPA ("The Sanctuary"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information when you join our waitlist.

2. The Data We Collect

When you sign up for our waitlist, we collect:

  • Email Address: To send you updates and early access links.
  • IP Address: For security and spam prevention purposes.
  • Consent Metadata: A timestamp of when you agreed to join the list.

3. Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process your data based on your Explicit Consent (Article 6(1)(a) of the UK/EU GDPR). You can withdraw this consent at any time by unsubscribing.

4. Third Parties

We use trusted partners to provide our services:

  • Supabase: Our database and backend infrastructure (AWS London region).
  • Resend: Our email delivery provider.

We do not sell or lease your data to any other third parties.

5. Retention

We store your email until you unsubscribe or until the service is officially launched and you choose not to create a full account. If the waitlist remains inactive for more than 2 years, we will delete your records.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise these rights, simply use the unsubscribe link in any email we send, or contact us.
